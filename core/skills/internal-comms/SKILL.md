# internal-comms

Compatibility shim. Internal comms drafting now lives in **`implement-plan`**; the `tech-writer` agent adds depth when installed.

→ Open `core/skills/implement-plan/SKILL.md` and follow that instead. Brief `tech-writer` if available.

This shim preserves the legacy trigger phrase during the migration release.

## If `implement-plan` is not available

Minimum viable fallback:

1. Name the audience first — teammates, leadership, or cross-functional
2. TL;DR at the top: the ask or the decision in one line
3. Concrete details below, ordered by what the reader needs to act
4. Date + owner + next action explicit
5. Convey, do not sell — internal audiences punish marketing voice
6. Surface risk and tradeoff openly; hidden risk burns trust
7. Keep it short; status updates over half a page do not get read
